About Us

In 2001, Richard Kwon founded Richard Kwon CPA upon relocating from Seoul, South Korea, to Seattle. Armed with a Bachelor's degree in accounting from Seoul University and a CPA license since 1998, Richard brings over two decades of expertise to the field. As a seasoned certified public accountant, he is dedicated to assisting individuals and businesses in optimizing their returns. Richard is not only fluent in both Korean and English but also embraces the opportunity to collaborate with individuals from diverse backgrounds and communities. His commitment to excellence and wealth of experience make him a trusted partner in navigating your financial success journey.
